LACDMH Help Line

Entry point for mental health services with the LA County department of mental health.

  • Referral to a service provider
  • Crisis counseling
  • Mobilizing field response teams
  • Linkages to other services and resources

  • 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. daily(option 3 when calling the Help Line)

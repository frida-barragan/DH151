These are some of the resources available for Los Angeles County residents that are dealing with mental health illness. In case of medical emergency, dial 911 immediately or go to the nearest emergency room.

  • Crisis counseling
  • Mobilizing field response teams

  • 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. daily(option 3 when calling the Help Line)

Some information about our map
The mental health facility data comes from the U.S. Department of Health & Human Services: Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration. It is limited in that services listed are either those sponsored/funded/administered by the government or those licensed by the state or officialized by another major entity. This thus neglects some community-based and non-traditional forms of mental health healing, especially for marginalized and alienated people and groups. The map also does not include individual private practices, which might be clustered in whiter, higher income neighborhoods. These gaps in the data make for an incomplete geospatial and urban analysis of mental health service accessibility.

With the mental health facility data, we juxtaposed demographic information for each zip code. This data comes from the 019 American Community Survey 5-Year Estimate Data Profiles.

Mental disorders explained


Any mental illness (AMI)
adults 18 and over who presently
have or have had a diagnosable mental,
emotional or behavioral disorder.

Serious mental illness (SMI)
adults 18 and over who presently
have or have had a functional-impairing mental,
emotional or behavioral disorder.

Serious emotional disturbance (SED)
children 17 and under who presently
have or have had a functional-impairing mental,
emotional or behavioral disorder.

Major depressive episode (MDE)
child/adult who presently
have depressed mood and a majority of
depressed symptoms.
